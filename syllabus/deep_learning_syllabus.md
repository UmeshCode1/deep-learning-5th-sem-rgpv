# Deep Learning Syllabus (AL 503(B)) — RGPV

AICTE Flexible Curricula — CSE (AI & ML) — V Semester

## Course Title
Deep Learning (AL 503(B))

## Unit I
- History of Deep Learning
- McCulloch-Pitts Neuron
- Multilayer Perceptrons (MLPs)
- Backpropagation
- Dimensionality reduction: PCA, SVD

## Unit II
- Optimization and Gradient Descent Variants (SGD, Momentum, RMSProp, Adam)
- Autoencoders and Denoising Autoencoders
- Variational Autoencoders (VAE)

## Unit III
- Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs)
- Architectures: LeNet, AlexNet, VGG, ResNet, GoogLeNet
- Regularization techniques: Dropout, BatchNorm, Data Augmentation

## Unit IV
- Recurrent Neural Networks (RNNs)
- LSTM, GRU
- Attention mechanisms and Transformers (overview)
- Applications: NLP, Sequence Modeling, Video Analytics

## Unit V
- Restricted Boltzmann Machines (RBMs) and Deep Belief Networks (DBNs)
- Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs)
- Other generative models and applications (image synthesis, data augmentation)

## Practical Work
- Implement basic models for each unit using Python and frameworks (TensorFlow / PyTorch)
- Evaluation metrics and visualization (accuracy, loss curves, confusion matrix)
